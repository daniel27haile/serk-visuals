Who We Are

At Serk Visuals, we believe every story deserves to be captured with clarity and creativity. We specialize in photography and videography — blending artistry with technology to deliver timeless visuals for weddings, events, portraits, brands, and real estate.

With years of experience and a genuine passion for visual storytelling, we work closely with every client to transform ideas into lasting memories. Whether it's a family portrait, a corporate event, or a cinematic wedding film, every detail is crafted with intention and delivered with pride.

We're proud to serve clients throughout the greater Seattle area — including Mill Creek, Everett, Lynnwood, Bellevue, Redmond, Kirkland, and Snohomish, Washington.
