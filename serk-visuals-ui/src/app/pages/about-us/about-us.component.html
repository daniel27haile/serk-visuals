About Serk Visuals

At Serk Visuals, we believe every story deserves to be captured with clarity and creativity. Specializing in photography and videography, we blend artistry with technology to deliver timeless visuals — from weddings and events to portraits, brands, and real estate.

With years of experience and a genuine passion for storytelling, we work closely with every client to transform ideas into lasting memories. Whether it’s a family portrait, a corporate event, or a cinematic wedding film, every detail is crafted with intention.

We’re proud to serve clients throughout the greater Seattle area — including Mill Creek, Everett, Lynnwood, Bellevue, Redmond, Kirkland, and Snohomish, Washington.
