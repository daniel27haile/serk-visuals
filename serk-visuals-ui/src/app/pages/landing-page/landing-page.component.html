FAQ

Frequently Asked Questions

How quickly will I receive my photos?

Standard delivery is 24–48 hours after the shoot. Rush delivery options are available — contact us for details.

How many photos are included?

The number of images depends on the property size. A typical listing receives 25–60 professionally edited photos. We'll confirm the expected count at booking.

What should homeowners do to prepare?

Declutter countertops and surfaces, open all blinds and curtains, turn on all interior lights, and park vehicles away from the front of the property. We'll handle everything else.

How are photos delivered?

You'll receive a download link with high-resolution, MLS-ready JPEG files formatted for web and print — ready to upload directly to your listing platform.

Do you provide property measurements or floor plans?

Yes. Accurate property measurements and 2D floor plan diagrams are available as an add-on. Select this during booking or contact us to add it to your order.

Do you photograph occupied homes?

Yes. We regularly photograph occupied listings and work efficiently in lived-in spaces. We'll share preparation guidelines in advance to ensure the best results.
